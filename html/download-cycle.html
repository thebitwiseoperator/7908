Download Cycle


Want more cycles? No problem! The NFC Tag Download Cycle1 feature allows you to select one of many additional cycles and download it directly to your Styler. You'll then be able to easily access it by selecting the “Download” option on your control panel.2

1Requires the free LG Smart Styler app for Android.
2The LG Styler holds up to two downloaded cycles.
